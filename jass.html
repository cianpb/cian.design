Jass

Film poster & packaging

The aim of this project was to design the title sequence, poster and promotional material for Jass, a graduate film from the National Film School at IADT.
 The dystopian narrative of the film revolves around humans wiping out animals in fear that they are becoming too intelligent. The irrational actions of the humans have led to technological regression. The visual language of the material reflects this in its mechanical, letterpressed style.
