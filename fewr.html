Fewr

UI/UX & Illustration

FEWR is a new initiative which aims to improve mental health and wellbeing by encouraging people to create more space at home and engage with their local community.


This multi-disciplinary project consists of a mobile app which takes the stress out of decluttering by offering users short, simple tasks to decide what items they do not need. FEWR also acts as a directory for users to find and book appointments with local charities and recycling centres for donations/drop-offs.
 By doing this, users have peace of mind that their personal space is clearer and their previous belongings have gone to the right place. As an added incentive, users earn points by donating items. These points can be redeemed for events, classes and activities in their local community.
